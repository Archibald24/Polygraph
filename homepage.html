What is click fraud
01. When you launch an ad campaign your ads are displayed on websites around the world.
02. Some of these websites are Controlled By Criminals
03. These criminals use technology & trickey to click on your ads.
04. For each of these clicks, they get a slice of your ad budget.
05. You cannot rely on the ad networks to protect you, as they get paid for every click too.
